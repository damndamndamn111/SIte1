    Jaw Crusher

    Sturdy housing construction
    Low operational costs
    Easy technical maintenance and operation

    Thanks to jaw crushers, even the toughest materials can be easily broken. The machine is easily adjustable with hydraulic systems depending on the desired production and productivity. This line of products, which has an automatic lubrication system, has been developed through superior engineering to provide excellent machine operation service. Jaw crushers are designed to crush any type of stone, regardless of its abrasiveness and hardness. High-performance Jaw Crushers break mountain rock and river gravel with movable jaws.
